About Me

Hello burger enthusiasts! I'm Jake, the voice behind "Burger, Fries, and a Jake." I started this blog to document my burger adventures, share my thoughts, work on my web development, and consume as many calories as possible in a single sitting.

While I'm much better at eating burgers than reviewing them, I hope you can glean some information about these scrumptious semi-sandwiches, and enjoy your time on this goofy-ass site.

The mission here is simple: review burgers in an unprofessional and frequently crass style while also providing an opportunity for me to work on web design. Based on that, I'd like to reiterate that this blog is all in good fun and not to be taken too seriously. If I dunk on your favorite burger, don't sweat it! Just remember that either you have terrible taste or it's just different!

And just to be crystal clear, reviews here won't follow any sort of style or particular rubric outside of my stream of consciousness (or VIBE!), so take it easy and have fun!

Lastly, this blog is a work-in-progress. If you see anything weird or have issues, please contact me either in the comments of a review page or at my e-mail (coming soon!). There will also be an updates page at somepoint, who knows when!
