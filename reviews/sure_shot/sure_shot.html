Welcome all to the first post of the blog! We are starting with a banger here at Sure Shot Burger, a smashburger patty foodcart up in Northeast Portland at 5013 Ne 42nd ave. They serve the standard style of smashburger, consisting of just a patty, pickles, onion, melted american cheese (thank fucking god), and homemade boirgur sauce! As a fan of the classic, I was really looking forward to trying this place out. It even has the Oakshire Pub right next door while you wait for your order. Excellent option considering how miserable it can get here in PDX around the holidays.

The food cart itself is festive and fun, mimicing the color and feeling of old diners in the 50s/60s.

I originally heard about Sure Shot from a buddy of mine (Mr. Big Kahuna iykyk) who knew about the (makings of) this burger blog and suggested it to me. Boy, oh boy, am I glad he did. I'll start off the review by stating this is likely in my top three burgers in the PDX region. Quite the heavy burger!
